Need Work Techniques?

Try using:

  • The Pomodoro Technique
    • The Pomodoro method organizes work and break times in order to be motivating and refreshing.
  • The Eisenhower Matrix
    • The Eisenhower Matrix is a way of categorizing and prioritizing tasks based on importance and urgency.
