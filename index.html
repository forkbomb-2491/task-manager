Pomodoro

  • Choose a task to focus on
  • Choose a length for worktime (recommended: 25-30 minutes)
  • Choose a length for breaktime (recommented: 5-10 minutes)
  • Switch between the two.

Minute Focus Block

  • Block out a larger time in your day to work on tasks, in order to minimize distractions for an extended period of time
  • Schedule 60-90 minute-long blocks during your most productive hours
