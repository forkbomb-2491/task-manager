Techniques

Pomodoro

  • Choose a task to focus on
  • Choose a length for worktime (recommended: 50 minutes)
  • Choose a length for breaktime (recommented: 5-10 minutes)
  • Switch between the two.

Frog

  • Identify Your Frog : figure out your most difficult tasks to work on. Have a look at your Task List for help.
  • Eat It : get them out of the way first thing in the morning, don't leave them for later
  • Repeat Every Day: repeat every day until it becomes a habit
